5 Things You Can Automate Today — In Under 10 Minutes Each

Why This Changes Everything

Most people think AI automation is complicated. It's not. You just need to start with the right 5 tasks. Each setup below takes under 10 minutes to build, runs automatically, and saves you hours every single week.

Automation #1: Your Morning Briefing (10 min setup)

What it does: Every morning, an AI agent reads your emails, checks your calendar, and sends you a single 200-word summary — before you've had coffee.

How to build it:

  1. Go to Claude.ai or ChatGPT
  2. Paste: "Act as my assistant. When I forward you emails and today's calendar, give me a 200-word morning briefing: what needs my attention, my schedule, and my #1 priority."
  3. Forward emails each morning → 2 minutes of reading instead of 45

Time saved: 30-45 min/day

Automation #2: Email Priority Filter (8 min setup)

What it does: AI reads every email and labels it URGENT / BATCH / DELETE. You only touch what actually needs you.

Prompt to use: "When I paste emails, label each: URGENT (reply today) / BATCH (can wait) / DELETE (no action). One label + 5-word reason per email."

Time saved: 1-2 hours/day

Automation #3: Social Media Caption Writer (5 min setup)

What it does: Paste any topic or video idea → get 3 captions, hashtags, and best posting time in 30 seconds.

Prompt to use: "You're a social media expert for an AI education account. For any topic I give you: write 3 captions (under 15 words each), 8 hashtags, and the best time to post."

Time saved: 2-3 hours/week

Automation #4: Follow-Up Tracker (7 min setup)

What it does: Never lose a lead. AI tracks your outreach and drafts follow-up messages for anyone who hasn't replied in 3+ days.

How: Keep a Google Sheet (Name | Date | Status). Weekly, paste it to AI with: "Draft a friendly follow-up for anyone with WAITING status contacted 3+ days ago."

Time saved: 3-5 hours/week

Automation #5: Content Repurposing Machine (6 min setup)

What it does: One piece of content → TikTok script + Instagram caption + email opener + LinkedIn post. All at once.

Prompt: "Turn this content into: (1) 60-sec TikTok script, (2) Instagram caption under 20 words, (3) 3-sentence email opener, (4) LinkedIn post under 150 words."

Time saved: 4-6 hours/week
