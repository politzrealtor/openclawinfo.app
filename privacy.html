Privacy Policy

Welcome to OpenClawInfo ("we," "us," or "our"). We operate the website openclawinfo.com and sell digital products, including the PDF guide "I Built an AI That Works While I Sleep." This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect information about you when you visit our site or purchase our products.

By using our website or purchasing our products, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

We do not operate our own checkout system. All purchases are processed through Gumroad, a third-party payment processor. When you buy our PDF, Gumroad collects your name, email address, and payment information directly. We receive only limited transactional data from Gumroad (e.g., your email address for order confirmation and delivery purposes).

If you contact us directly via email at hello@openclawinfo.com, we collect your email address and any information you voluntarily include in your message.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit openclawinfo.com, certain information is collected automatically, including:

  • Your IP address (anonymized where possible)
  • Browser type and version
  • Device type (mobile, desktop, tablet)
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited, time on page, and referring URL
  • Date and time of your visit

This information is collected via cookies and similar technologies (see Section 3).

1.3 Information We Do Not Collect

We do not knowingly collect sensitive personal data such as government IDs, financial account details, health information, or data from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us immediately.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Order fulfillment: To deliver your purchased PDF and send purchase confirmation emails via Gumroad.
  • Customer support: To respond to questions, complaints, and requests sent to hello@openclawinfo.com.
  • Website improvement: To understand how visitors use our site and improve its content, design, and performance.
  • Analytics: To measure traffic, conversion rates, and user behavior in aggregate form.
  • Legal compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes.
  • Fraud prevention: To detect and prevent fraudulent or abusive activity on our site.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not use your information for automated decision-making that produces legal or similarly significant effects on you.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to operate properly and to understand how visitors interact with our content. Cookies are small text files placed on your device.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential cookies: Required for the website to function. These cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand which pages are visited most often and how users navigate the site. Data is aggregated and anonymized.
  • Marketing cookies: May be set by third-party advertising platforms (including TikTok Pixel and Meta/Instagram Pixel — see Section 4) to enable us to understand the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns.

Managing Cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies at any time via your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect website functionality. For more information about managing cookies, visit allaboutcookies.org.

4. Third-Party Services

We work with the following third-party services that may collect or process your data:

4.1 Gumroad

All purchases are processed by Gumroad, Inc. Gumroad collects your payment details, email address, and billing information directly. Gumroad's handling of your data is governed by their own Privacy Policy. We recommend reviewing it before completing a purchase.

4.2 TikTok

We may use the TikTok Pixel on our website to measure the effectiveness of our TikTok advertising campaigns. TikTok may collect information about your visit (including pages viewed and actions taken) and use it to show you relevant ads on the TikTok platform. TikTok's data practices are governed by TikTok's Privacy Policy.

4.3 Instagram / Meta

We may use the Meta Pixel (Instagram/Facebook) to understand traffic from our Instagram presence and to run retargeting advertisements. Meta may collect device identifiers, browsing behavior, and other signals. Meta's data practices are governed by Meta's Privacy Policy.

4.4 Analytics Providers

We may use website analytics tools such as Plausible Analytics, Fathom, or similar privacy-respecting platforms to measure site traffic. These tools collect anonymized, aggregate data and do not use cookies that track individuals across the web.

4.5 Web Hosting & CDN

Our website may be hosted on platforms such as Vercel, Netlify, or Cloudflare. These providers may log access requests including your IP address in accordance with their own privacy policies.

You may request deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us at hello@openclawinfo.com. Note that we may be required to retain certain records by law.
